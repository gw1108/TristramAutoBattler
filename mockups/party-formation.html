Call to Arms — Party Formation

After ringing the bell, adventurers form up to 3 parties of 1-3 in the world. Clicking a hero opens their normal hero stat panel with one extra section at the bottom: a "Party:" label, Kick and Swap buttons, and the actions-remaining counter — all on one horizontal row. Actions per day are 2 by default, raised to 3 by the War Table upgrade at the inn. Kick disables when there is no benched adventurer to swap in (town total ≤ 9). Swap with no valid target (e.g. only one party is non-empty) is a no-op and does not consume an action.

Two inn upgrades change formation: Guild Champion (inn 2) seeds the highest-level adventurer into a random party before the shuffle, so your best is never benched. Seasoned Replacements (inn 1) triples the weight of reserves within ±1 level of the target party's average or highest when Kick rolls a replacement.
