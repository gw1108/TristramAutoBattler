Call to Arms — Party Formation

After ringing the bell, adventurers form up to 3 parties of 1-3 in the world. Clicking a hero opens their normal hero stat panel with one extra section at the bottom: a "Party:" label, Kick and Swap buttons, and the actions-remaining counter — all on one horizontal row. The number of actions per day comes from the player's stats (default 3). Kick disables when there is no benched adventurer to swap in (town total ≤ 9).
