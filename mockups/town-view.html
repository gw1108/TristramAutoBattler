Town View & Interact Prompts

Fake 960×540 viewport. There is no HUD in the town — the only UI is the gold proximity prompt that appears when the player is near an interactable (the same UIs also open via left-click + auto-pathfind). The treasure chest is the exception: its prompt is a readout, not an action, and needs no interact — approaching it is how the player checks their gold. Emoji are placeholder world sprites.

Five buildings: the inn (starts built at level 1) plus three separate equipment shops — weapon, armor, jewelry, one per gear slot — and the training grounds. All four non-inn buildings start ruined at 10g each, so the player's first real decision is which one to rebuild. The three shops need to read as distinct trades at a glance (forge / armourer / jeweller), since the player picks between them constantly.
