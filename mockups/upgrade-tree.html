Building Upgrade UI — all 5 buildings

Opened by clicking a building's door (or approach + interact). One panel per building. Pips show current/max tier of each upgrade. Locked rows require a higher building level. Ruined buildings show only a rebuild action. Costs scale: building levels go 40 / 100 / 250 / 625 (×2.5 each), and each pip costs base × 1.8^(pips owned), so the price shown on a row is always the price of the next pip.

The town has five buildings: the inn (starts at level 1), three equipment shops — weapon, armor, jewelry, one per gear slot — and the training grounds. Everything except the inn starts ruined at 10g to rebuild.

Each shop's building level is its gear-tier unlock — there is no "Unlock Equipment Tier" pip line. Level 1 stocks tiers 1-2, level 2 adds 3-4, level 3 adds tier 5, and levels 4 and 5 unlock that shop's two shop-wide lines (weapon = offense, armor = defense, jewelry = morale). That is why a shop tree has no pips at all until level 4; the panel should handle that empty state gracefully rather than looking broken. The three shops below are deliberately shown at different levels to display every state at once.
