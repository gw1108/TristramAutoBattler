Tutorial Panels (all 5)

Shared layout: title + screenshot image + instruction text + close button, vertically stacked, center aligned. Striped boxes are placeholder screenshots; yellow text marks the in-image arrow callouts.

Starting Out
screenshot: player next to the rooster Crow! ⟶
Move with W,A,S,D.
Interact with left-click or E.
You can left-click on heroes for more details.

Next step: Wake the rooster on the left to start the day and call heroes to the inn.
Entering the Dungeon
screenshot: player next to the dungeon entrance + bell ⟵ Ring!
Interact to call to arms for an expedition. After parties have assembled you can suggest 2 changes by clicking the heroes. Then enter the dungeon.
Dungeon Runs
screenshot: close-up of heroes fighting enemies
Heroes will navigate on their own.
They level up and collect gold and items.
If their morale is critical they may cower — or flee.
Upgrade Buildings
screenshot: ruined starting building Rebuild — 10g ⟶
You can upgrade buildings by interacting with them. Upgraded buildings improve your adventurers!
Summoning Heroes
screenshot: the inn with heroes + player
Wake the rooster each day to call new heroes to the inn.
Recruit as many heroes as you can afford!
