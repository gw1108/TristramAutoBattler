Expedition Summary

Post-dungeon panel sized to ~80% of the window in both dimensions; the results area scrolls vertically if it doesn't fit. Three equal-width columns, one per party, each reserving vertical space for 3 adventurers. Above each party: a visual of the dungeon level it traversed (level 1 = gentle grassy forest, level 2 = swampy forest) — parties pick the hardest unlocked level fitting their character levels, so columns can show different biomes in one expedition. Per living member: current level banner → XP bar → next level banner → coin pile (1 coin per 3-6 gold, min 1, cap 100; hidden at 0). Dead members show no XP bar and no gold.
